Corporate Ties

Looping

Looping is a high intensity customised volume training workout that specifically targets boosting your cardio fitness. In looping, you spin the pedals repeatedly to reach the cardio peak and then ease back down. This full body workout is a great way to lift your personal performance in the group, as you draw on the group’s energy, looping to the rhythm of music. The instructor driven strategy and the music helps you monitor resistance levels and speed.

MMA

MMA (Mix Martial Arts) is a full body workout that pushes your body to the limit like no other sport while working your upper body, lower body and core. It is also a high intensity workout that challenges your cardiovascular muscles and burns calories quickly. You can burn up to 590 calories in a single 45-minute session. Overall, MMA workouts incorporate strength, aerobic and metabolic conditioning, so you gain endurance, strength, agility, and power.

Calisthenics

Calisthenics is a form of physical exercise, which utilizes one’s own body weight for strength training. Herein, through bodyweight training exercises we focus on strengthening the core.The workout involves complex movements involving training different muscle groups. The workout includes exercises like human flags,push ups,pull ups,and muscle ups.Individuals of all age groups can do calisthenics,but,it cannot be done by beginners who haven’t worked out.

HIIT

High Intensity Interval Training with variations in workouts focuses on different aspects of fitness i.e. strength,stamina,& endurance.Each class is designed based on the format that is created by a panel of experts. With the potential to torch maximum calories in a minimum amount of time,The HIIT is all about doing short periods of all-out work followed by short periods of active rest. This makes the body work harder than it does during steady-state cardio.

Yoga

The benefits that your body can reap from yoga are multifold; contrary to popular belief it does much more than increase your flexibility. Yoga also increases muscle strength and tone, improves respiration, heart health, circulatory health, athletic performance and energy and vitality. The way we teach yoga at Multifit, we go beyond all of this and work on improving your will-power and make you fit both physically and mentally.

Rehab-Training

Rehab training helps in recovery of injuries, and in preventing further injuries. In this scientific- based program, the injuries are assessed first, after which personalized exercises are given to you improve mobility and helps in athletic performance as well as functional movements. The training helps an individual stay in shape through variation of functional exercises and helps in mobility which is basically improving the range of motion.

Functional Training

Functional training simply means training your body to perform everyday movements such as lifting and climbing stairs.The time spent training with functional equipment in open spaces makes life better by developing your agility,flexibility and dynamic strength.The training can be undertaken by anyone from a beginner to an advanced level.

Circuit Training

Circuit training is a style of body conditioning workout. It, in fact, is a series of strength and cardio exercises where you cycle through several exercises and with minimal rest in between target different muscle groups. Since you're alternating the body part you're working during each move, there is minimal rest between exercises. This challenging workout taxes your muscle strength builds endurance and your cardiorespiratory system.

Strength and conditioning

This program focuses on strength and conditioning, which helps in improving a skill in a sport. It strengthens core stability and improves speed, endurance, and athletic performance. The strength and conditioning program uses exercise prescription specifically for the improvement of performance in athletic competition and thus can be done by all age groups including teens and athletes.

About Us

Conceived in the UK in July 2015, MultiFit has rapidly spread it’s footprint across India, UK and the UAE. Born as a 3000 sq ft functional fitness studio at Kalyani Nagar Pune, MultiFit as a result of the passion of its promoters, the husband-wife duo of Samir Kapoor & Sally Jones and its highly energized team, has gained a strong reputation of not just being one of the most technically sound fitness studio chains in India but also the country’s largest Functional Fitness Studio brand. Recently awarded the FEA award for Excellence in Multi-Dsicipline Training, MultiFit has 31 large-format fitness studios in 11 cities across 3 countries in a short span of just under 3 Years

A perfect antidote to boring gyms, Multifit is focused on cross Functional Training, a methodology which involves training the body for cardio and fitness using activities performed in daily life. People who have tried it have found it addictive, as they are able to see results without hating their workouts. The core idea is to cut out the entire monotony from the session to make it as enticing as your favourite sport
