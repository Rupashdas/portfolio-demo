About Us

MultiFit aims at changing the definition of fitness from mere ‘Body Building’ to ‘Strength Building’. Keeping to this belief we offer a wide variety of unconventional workouts where patrons can try their hand at Calisthenics, Boxing, Dancing, Jeet Kune Do, Mixed Martial Arts and many more workouts which ensure that your sessions are never boring.
