Pyrrhic Re is a Reinsurance Business designed to achieve an excellent result at low costs.

who we are

Pyrrhic Re Ltd. was established in 2019 in London as a Managing General Agency (MGA) writing a diversified and balanced book of treaty and facultative reinsurance business on a proportional and non-proportional basis.

Pyrrhic Re Guernsey Ltd. will be established as a Protected Cell Company (PCC) in 2019. Pyrrhic Re PCC will provide quota share reinsurance to our Risk Capacity providers. and provide our investors the opportunity to invest in our portfolio with risk capacity support.

Our Offerings

Property:
  • Treaty catastrophe exposed quota share and excess of loss.
  • Single peak zone retrocession excess of loss.
  • International facultative reinsurance.
Casualty:
  • Treaty umbrella, professional Lines and cyber quota share and excess of loss.
Speciality:
  • Treaty offshore and onshore energy, marine hull & cargo, aviation, agriculture and specialty other quota share and excess of loss.
To Our Clients & Reinsurance Brokers:
  • Pyrrhic Re shall be a Target Cedant Underwriter of market leading insurance businesses. Pyrrhic Re will not write across the market and support your competitors.
  • Pyrrhic Re shall be supported by A rated AM Best paper or better on the back of MGA agreement terms of 24 months or longer. We are here for the long term.
  • We see Pyrrhic Re as being successful on the back of being nimble and selective in risk, low in expense and re insurer only in message. We do not support our target client’s competitors or compete directly against our clients via Insurance platforms.
  • Pyrrhic Re Embraces the “new normal” reinsurance market Future market cycles will not fluctuate as much as they have in past and margins will be hard earned on risk selection and cost control.
To Our Capacity Providers:
  • Pyrrhic Re shall be provided with risk capacity from re/insurers with an AM Best Financial Strength Rating of A or better. Our capacity providers will partner with Pyrrhic Re because:
  • The strength and track record of the Underwriting team at Pyrrhic Re.
  • The desire to diversify a regional, life or monoline existing reinsurance portfolio with accretive and profitable P&C reinsurance business.
  • They are Insurers who wish to access the reinsurance market without having the expenditure of a company buildout.
  • The belief that the market reputation of the Pyrrhic Re executive team will allow those markets better market signings.
  • They conclude that Pyrrhic Re can underwrite a reinsurance portfolio buildout with better results and at a lower expense base than anyone else.
To Our Investors :
  • The Pyrrhic Re investor has taken the decision to support a qualitative underwriting team who shall harness their 70 years of underwriting experience to deliver a sustainable portfolio with market outperforming results. Our investors applaud our low expense platform and the desire to achieve one of the reinsurance markets lowest expense ratios.
  • Our investors applaud the long-term vision of Pyrrhic Re. To buildout a Direct Authorized U.K. MGA over time whose business is closely tied to the Pyrrhic Re brand and values. Pyrrhic Re also strives to develop a rated entity from our Guernsey PCC vehicle.
  • The business growth will likely ultimate in 5 years. At that stage, Pyrrhic shall not expand offices, staff or product lines. So many reinsurance competitors have gone this direction unsuccessfully. With its investors support, Pyrrhic Re shall stick to its core competence and capabilities in order to deliver superior returns.
